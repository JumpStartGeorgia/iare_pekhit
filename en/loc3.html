The Left Embankment

Site Location: The Slope of Vakhushti Bridge (on the side of Didube)

The proposed installation must be produced within the red borders drawn around the project plan. It is optional whether you choose to cover the whole marked territory for your installation. No changes may be made to any existing trees, plantings, or landscape elements. The installation must be respectful to pedestrians, drivers and vehicles. It shall not impose a threat to the existing environment or create obstacles.

We collaborate with the relevant departments of the Tbilisi City Hall and assist the artists in the technical aspects of project placement. We can help provide a power source, or other technical support as required throughout the project.

Application Deadline: March 4, 2015 00:00
