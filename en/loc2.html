Hero Square

Iare Pekhit wants YOU to take part in the public art project “Uare Qalaqs” (Care for the City). Are you going to be 1 of the 5 winning artists to present your street installation in one of the five chosen public areas of Tbilisi?

For our project, we are looking for artists, who can transform the given spaces and play with the way the inhabitants see and feel the city. Artists, who are able to drag the spectator out of their comfort zones and have them collaborate, initiate and develop new things together.

Site Location: Hero Square, Area In Front of the Circus Building Staircase

Application Deadline: March 4, 2015 00:00
